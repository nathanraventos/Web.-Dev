FAQ

How experienced do you need to be to start river rafting?

No prior experience is necessary to start river rafting, as there are beginner-friendly trips with mild rapids and experienced guides providing instruction. For intermediate rivers, some prior rafting experience or good physical condition may be beneficial. Advanced and expert-level rivers require more experience, with participants needing advanced rafting skills and the ability to handle challenging conditions.

What equipment do you need in order to river raft?

For river rafting, essential equipment includes a raft, paddles, life jackets, and helmets. It's crucial to wear appropriate clothing, such as quick-drying and moisture-wicking gear, and sturdy water shoes. Depending on the trip and conditions, additional gear like wetsuits, drysuits, and splash jackets may be recommended for comfort and safety.
